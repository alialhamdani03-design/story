Story

Private TikTok draft workflow

Story is a desktop workflow used by its owner to prepare original or properly licensed vertical videos and upload them to TikTok as drafts for final review.

How it works

  • The account owner connects their TikTok account through TikTok Login Kit.
  • The tool uploads selected videos through TikTok's Content Posting API.
  • Uploaded content is reviewed as a TikTok draft before posting.
