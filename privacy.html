Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Story is a private desktop content workflow. It uses TikTok Login Kit and TikTok's Content Posting API to let the account owner upload selected videos as TikTok drafts.

Information used

The workflow may use TikTok authorization tokens, TikTok open ID information, selected local video files, captions, hashtags, and upload status information.

How information is used

Information is used only to authorize the account owner and upload selected content to TikTok as drafts or approved posts.

Passwords

The workflow does not collect, request, or store TikTok passwords. Authorization is handled through TikTok's OAuth flow.

Sharing

Information is not sold. Data is shared with TikTok only as needed to authorize the account and upload selected content through TikTok's official APIs.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app owner.
